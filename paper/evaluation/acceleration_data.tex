\subsection{Data Aggregation} \label{acceleration_data}

We employed a Wii Remote\texttrademark~Plus controller to record different gestures for multiple users. Each user performed 8 gestures, first in a controlled environment to record clean training samples and afterwards in noisy environment to record a test time series stream, which includes all predetermined gestures as well as acceleration data that corresponds to other physical activities.
All records are available for download on our project website \cite{lesti2017filter}. A sample record containing both training and test gestures is illustrated in Figure \ref{fig:record}.
